Most recent project.

Bolhuis Website

For school I had to make a website for a company (commission competition), we had 3 different choices and I went with the Bolhuis tree nursery. The website is made with HTML, CSS and Bootstrap to make it responsive. The images used are from the original Bolhuis website and material we received from them to work with. I worked with a group of 4 people to make this website we originally planned to do a game as well but due to there being a split in our team between game and web developers we decided to focus on websites mainly. I made the homepage and the contact page after our web developer became unavailable halfway throughout the week, we finished it in time and the owner of the company was happy with the website, we sadly didn't get first place but it was a good learning expirience.
